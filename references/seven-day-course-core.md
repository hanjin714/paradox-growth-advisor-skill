## Day 1: 小团队别先学 AI，先看钱漏在哪

Core judgment:

```text
AI 是放大器，不是印钞机。
小团队第一步不是买系统、搭智能体、追模型，而是先看客户、线索、跟进、复购到底散在哪里。
```

### What This Day Solves

Day 1 is a business checkup, not a tool class.

Many small teams think they have an AI problem, but the real issue is simpler:

- A customer asked, but nobody recorded it.
- A conversation happened, but no next action exists.
- A customer bought once, but no one appears at the next service or replenishment node.
- The boss remembers some things, employees remember others, and nothing becomes shared business memory.

The first question is not “which AI tool should I use?”

The first question is:

```text
钱到底漏在哪？
```

### Three Money Leaks

| Leak | What It Looks Like | First Public Fix |
|-|-|-|
| Lead leak | Comments, private messages, calls, or inquiries are not recorded | Put real inquiries into one shared table |
| Follow-up leak | After talking, no owner, next step, or follow-up date exists | Add owner, next action, and next follow-up date |
| Repeat-purchase leak | Existing customers have cycles, but no reminder or reactivation node exists | Record service/product cycle and next trigger |

Examples of repeat-purchase or follow-up nodes:

| Industry | Public Examples |
|-|-|
| Pet/local retail | Deworming, vaccination, grooming, supplies replenishment |
| Education service | Trial class, renewal, level-up, vacation timing |
| Home improvement / smart home | Before water-electric work, before move-in, old-house upgrade |
| Local services | Service cycle, return visit, next care |
| B2B services | Renewal, upgrade, new project, next meeting |

### Five Questions Every Industry Must Answer

Industries differ. Fields differ. The bottom layer is similar.

Ask:

```text
客户是谁？
现在到哪一步？
下一步干什么？
谁负责？
什么时候跟？
```

If these five questions cannot be answered, the customer is still scattered.

Use these public lines:

```text
不是没有客户，是客户散了。
不是没有数据，是数据睡着了。
```

### Real Assets Are AI Fuel

AI can only amplify real assets:

- Real customer questions.
- Real deal cases.
- Real store/service scenes.
- Real service processes.
- Employee experience.
- Historical conversations.
- Repeat-purchase records.
- Frequently asked questions.

Without these, AI produces a shell: correct-looking content without business roots.

### First Table Before First Agent

The first table is not an admin task. It is the business entry point for AI.

Minimum public fields:

| Field | Why It Exists |
|-|-|
| Date | When the lead appeared |
| Source platform | Where the person came from |
| Name / handle | How to find them again |
| Region | Whether they match the service range |
| Need | What they asked |
| Stage | Where they are now |
| Budget | Whether the direction roughly matches |
| Materials sent | What has already been given |
| Lead grade | A/B/C |
| Owner | Who follows up |
| Next action | What happens next |
| Next follow-up date | When to act again |

The table must answer:

```text
今天来了哪些新线索？
哪些线索最值得跟？
每条线索下一步是什么？
```

### A/B/C Is Attention Protection

A lead:

- In service range.
- Clear need.
- Willing to continue.
- Has next action.
- Willing to send materials, book time, or discuss budget.

B lead:

- Interested but incomplete.
- Budget, time, need, or stage is unclear.
- Suitable for nurturing, examples, materials, or follow-up questions.

C lead:

- Not matched.
- Only asks lowest price.
- No clear need.
- No next step.

Public line:

```text
分层不是怠慢客户，分层是保护团队精力。
```

### Daily Eight Numbers

Do not only watch views.

Watch:

| Number | Diagnosis |
|-|-|
| Posts published | Did the team act today? |
| Views | Was the content seen? |
| Comments | Did users open their mouth? |
| Private messages | Did it enter next-step communication? |
| Next-step conversations | Did a real dialogue happen? |
| A leads | Did valuable leads appear? |
| Handed-off leads | Did front-end pass to back-end? |
| Result feedback | Did results return to records? |

Use this diagnostic chain:

```text
有播放没评论，可能内容太泛，没有开口点。
有评论没私信，可能行动提示不清楚。
有私信没下一步，可能承接或资料入口弱。
有沟通没 A 类线索，可能内容吸引错人。
有 A 类线索没转交，可能团队交接断了。
有转交没成交反馈，可能后端结果没有回流。
```

Build the first lead table and fill 10 real inquiries.

Do not invent. Use real comments, messages, calls, employee notes, order records, or customer memories.


## Day 2: 本地门店和小团队，7 天到底拍什么

Core judgment:

```text
内容不是作品，是线索筛选器。
7 天不是日历，是用户从陌生到开口的路径。
```

### What This Day Solves

Many teams “post content” but do not get business signals.

The problem is often not that the content is ugly. The problem is that the content does not let the right person know:

- Am I the audience?
- What problem does this business solve?
- Can I trust the process?
- What should I ask next?
- Will my question become a record or just disappear?

### Three Bad Content Types

| Type | What It Looks Like | Why It Fails | Fix |
|-|-|-|-|
| 泛 | “专注品质服务”“用心做好每个细节” | Anyone can read it, nobody feels named | Add object, scene, contradiction |
| 炫 | Equipment, systems, jargon, certificates | Peers understand, customers do not know relevance | Translate expertise into customer judgment |
| 断 | Content is interesting but leads nowhere | User does not know what to comment or ask | Add a concrete opening point |

Public line:

```text
用户评论你，不一定是因为你讲得完整，而是因为你把他的状态讲具体了。
```

### Content Flywheel

Use this flywheel:

```text
看见 → 理解 → 信任 → 开口 → 记录 → 复盘
```

| Step | User State | Content Job |
|-|-|-|
| 看见 | First knows you exist | Say who you are, where you are, who you serve |
| 理解 | Knows you are not generic | Name the problem you solve |
| 信任 | Feels you have real process | Show process, cases, standards |
| 开口 | Comments, asks, gives their situation | Plant a concrete question |
| 记录 | Question does not stay in comments | Put it into lead table |
| 复盘 | Learn which content works | Feed real problems into next content |

### 7-Day Content Compass

| Day | Content Role | What To Shoot | User Psychology | Goal |
|-|-|-|-|-|
| Day 1 | Existence | Who you are, where you are, who you serve | “原来你在这里” | Let the right person see you |
| Day 2 | Pain | Specific customer struggle | “这说的不就是我吗” | Let user identify themselves |
| Day 3 | Case/process | A real handling process | “这个人不是只会卖” | Build trust |
| Day 4 | Avoid pit / standard | How to judge a solution | “原来不能只问价格” | Give decision standard |
| Day 5 | Repeat purchase | Why old customers return | “这不是一单结束” | Surface mature needs |
| Day 6 | Process | Reception, record, follow-up, feedback | “来了以后不是没人管” | Show team reliability |
| Day 7 | Q&A | Answer top questions from previous days | “我也有这个问题” | Turn real questions into new content |

### Five Material Libraries

| Material Type | What To Use | Role |
|-|-|-|
| Scene | Store, workspace, service scene, delivery scene | Prove real existence |
| Problem | Customer FAQ, employee repeated explanations | Let users identify themselves |
| Process | Judgment, handling, delivery, review | Build professional trust |
| Comparison | Different choices and outcomes | Help customer judge |
| Node | Repeat purchase, maintenance, revisit, upgrade | Connect content to long-term business |

### Every Content Piece Needs An Opening Point

Opening point means giving users a step to speak.

Examples:

| Content Type | Ending Question |
|-|-|
| Avoid pit | 你现在最担心的是价格、效果，还是后续没人管？ |
| Case | 你想看哪个行业的版本？ |
| Process | 你们团队现在最乱的是记录、跟进，还是复盘？ |
| Pain | 你现在卡在哪一步？ |

### Every Content Piece Must Fit A Field

Before publishing, ask:

```text
这条内容如果有人评论，我希望得到哪类信息？
```

Possible lead-table fields:

- Source.
- Industry.
- Region.
- Pain point.
- Budget.
- Stage.
- Repeat-purchase cycle.
- Owner.
- Next action.

If the answer cannot be written into a field, the content may only be noise.

### Day 2 Homework

Create a 7-day content table:

| Field | Meaning |
|-|-|
| Date | Which day |
| Content role | Existence, pain, case, avoid pit, repeat purchase, process, Q&A |
| Topic | What the content says |
| Target audience | Who it is for |
| Customer question | What the user is struggling with |
| Material | Store, case, process, comparison, comment |
| First sentence | How to stop the user |
| Opening point | What the user can ask |
| Lead field | Which field the comment can enter |
| Review metric | Comment quality, inquiry quality, or repeat-purchase question |

Public reserve:

```text
Do not reveal platform evasion, gray traffic tactics, complete account growth tactics, or private conversion design.
```

## Day 3: 从内容到线索记录

Core judgment:

```text
有人开口，不等于你有线索。
开口不是线索，记录后才算进入业务。
```

### What This Day Solves

Day 1 found the leak. Day 2 created content openings. Day 3 solves the next problem:

```text
客户开口之后，怎么不让机会睡在评论区、聊天框、员工手机、老板脑子里。
```

### Reply And Record Are Not The Same

| Action | Meaning | Problem |
|-|-|-|
| Reply | Send a polite response | Relationship exists, but business may not |
| Record | Capture source, original words, stage, owner, next action | Can hand off, review, and continue |

Example:

User says:

```text
我们店里就十来个人，员工根本不愿意拍，怎么办？
```

Reply:

```text
可以先从老板拍起。
```

Record:

| Field | Content |
|-|-|
| Source | Day2 content: 7 天到底拍什么 |
| Scene | Ten-person store |
| Original words | 员工根本不愿意拍 |
| Current stage | Problem clear, industry not filled |
| Owner | A specific person |
| Next action | Ask industry, whether they have posted before, stuck at filming or script |
| Deadline | Tomorrow noon |

Public line:

```text
前者是礼貌，后者才是机会不丢。
```

### Four Sleeping Places

| Place | What Happens |
|-|-|
| Comment section | User asks, operator replies, no record exists |
| Chat box | Need, budget, timing, and concern are exposed but stay in one phone |
| Employee retelling | “这个客户我跟着呢” but nobody knows where it is |
| Boss memory | Early stage works, later becomes the team's most expensive and fragile database |

Danger sentence:

```text
这个人好像问过。
```

When “好像” appears, source, problem, owner, and next step have already started disappearing.

### Six-Step Lead Flow

```text
开口 → 记录 → 补全 → 判断 → 动作 → 反馈
```

These six steps answer:

```text
这个人为什么会出现在这里？
他现在处在哪一步？
谁在什么时候做下一步？
```

### Do Not Write Fake Actions

| Vague | Executable |
|-|-|
| 继续跟进 | 明天 14:00 前补问行业和团队人数 |
| 保持联系 | 周五前发送两种方案差异，并确认是否需要预约沟通 |
| 有空问问 | 今天 21:00 前补问预算范围和使用场景 |

### Minimum Lead Record

Start with fields employees are willing to fill:

| Field | What To Record |
|-|-|
| Time | When the lead appeared |
| Source | Which content, live, or entrance |
| Customer info | Handle, region, industry, team size |
| Original question | Keep real words |
| Current stage | Just opened, problem clear, info missing, booked, etc. |
| Owner | One clear person |
| Next action | Ask, send, confirm, book, revisit |
| Deadline | Specific date and time |

### Keep Customer Original Words

Do not immediately translate customer words into professional terms.

Customer says:

```text
员工不愿意拍，一拍就像背稿。
```

Do not only write:

```text
企业内容生产机制缺失。
```

Original words matter because:

1. The next person can understand the real scene quickly.
2. They become future content topics.
3. They are real material for AI classification, script generation, and pain-point extraction.

### Do Not Only Write “Interested”

Use factual stages:

| Stage | Meaning |
|-|-|
| 刚开口 | User left one question or signal |
| 问题明确 | We know where they are stuck |
| 信息待补 | Missing industry, budget, timing, team size, or key facts |
| 已预约下一步 | Communication, store visit, trial, material send, or appointment is set |
| 方案判断中 | Comparing plans or waiting for internal confirmation |
| 暂不推进 | Not matched, low relevance, or currently watching |

### Team Handoff

“你跟一下” is not handoff.

A real handoff includes:

- Source.
- Original words.
- Current stage.
- Next action.
- Deadline.

Goal:

```text
让接手的人不用重新考古客户。
```

### AI Entry

AI can:

- Turn comments into lead candidates.
- Suggest missing facts.
- Help classify A/B/C.
- Summarize weekly high-frequency questions.
- Turn records back into content topics.

But run manually first:

```text
先人工记录 10 条、20 条、30 条。
先发现哪些字段真的有人填，哪些字段只是老板想象出来的。
```

## Day 4: 爆款不是终点，A 类线索才是

Core judgment:

```text
播放量告诉你内容有没有被看见，咨询说明用户愿意开口，A 类线索才告诉团队：现在应该把时间放在谁身上，以及下一步具体做什么。
```

### What This Day Solves

Records alone are not enough. The team must share a judgment standard.

Day 4 translates “感觉不错”“好像有意向” into observable facts.

### Three Different Signals

| Signal | Shows | Does Not Prove |
|-|-|-|
| Views | Content was seen | Viewers are your customers |
| Inquiry | User is willing to leave a signal | It deserves heavy manual attention |
| A lead | Object, problem, and next action are clearer | It will definitely close |

High-view content may be bad business content. Low-view content may produce precise leads.

### A/B/C Is Not Customer Ranking

Public line:

```text
A/B/C 不是客户高低分，而是团队注意力分配。
```

| Type | Current State | Action |
|-|-|-|
| A | Matched object, specific problem, willingness to continue, clear next step | Assign owner, action, and time quickly |
| B | Real problem but missing information | Ask missing facts and rejudge |
| C | Vague, unmatched, or watching | Keep record, use public content to hold, do not consume too much manual time |

C can change later. It is not abandonment. It is current attention allocation.

### Do Not Use “Interested” As Judgment

Check five observable facts:

| Fact | Team Question |
|-|-|
| Service match | Is region, industry, team size, or service range matched? |
| Problem specificity | Is it a real scene or just “想用 AI”? |
| Information sufficiency | Do we know status, user, timing, or site conditions? |
| Willingness | Will they add info, view materials, or schedule? |
| Next action | Can we write appointment, question, site check, or material send? |

Say less:

```text
这个客户感觉能拿下。
```

Say more:

```text
他为什么匹配，现在缺什么，谁在什么时候完成下一步。
```

### Three Industry Examples

Local store:

```text
Nearby, clear service need, recent timing, and visit action are stronger signals than a generic price question.
```

Enterprise service / AI implementation:

```text
“我们对 AI 感兴趣” is not A.
“团队 8 个人，咨询分散在几个平台和员工手机里，想先把线索统一起来，周三可以梳理” is closer.
```

Smart home:

```text
“智能家居多少钱” is not enough.
“已入住，想解决老人房夜间起身看不清” is a specific scene.
```

### Complete Progression Flow

```text
内容被看见 → 用户开口 → 保留原话与来源 → 补全判断事实 → 判断 A/B/C → 明确负责人、动作、时间 → 结果回填并重新判断
```

The label is not the point. The action behind the label is the point.

### AI Boundary

AI can suggest categories and reasons after the team has rules.

Use this order:

1. Record real leads manually.
2. Business owner reviews classification and exceptions.
3. After standards stabilize, let Agent assist sorting and suggestions.
4. Put frequent stable reminders into workflow.
5. When the ability needs a long-term internal page, use Codex.

3. Mark each as A/B/C with one reason.
4. Every A lead must have:

| Required | Example |
|-|-|
| Owner | A specific person |
| Next action | Ask team size and current platform before tomorrow 14:00 |
| Completion time | A date/time |
| Result feedback | Return to lead table |

## Day 5: Agent、workflow、Codex 怎么选

Core judgment:

```text
临时判断用 Agent，固定重复用 workflow，需要做成长期工具或系统再用 Codex。
但前提是：先把业务动作写清楚。
```

### What This Day Solves

The wrong assumption:

```text
先选一个最强工具，再让它解决所有问题。
```

The actual rule:

```text
工具不是信仰，也不是排名。工具要按业务动作选择。
```

### Three Tool Metaphors

| Tool | Like | Suitable For | Not Suitable For |
|-|-|-|-|
| Agent | Temporary consultant | Messy input, sorting, extracting, suggesting next step | Final commitments, risk judgment, guaranteed outcomes |
| Workflow | Fixed conveyor belt | Stable repeated steps, clear input/output, timed trigger | Rules change every day |
| Codex | Long-term business tool | Page, permission, search, status board, internal system | Building a product from unclear workflow |

### Three Tool Questions

1. Is this task different every time?

Use Agent when inputs vary and need context:

- Ten customer questions in different wording.
- Comments include price, effect, complaint, or vague interest.
- Need to know what information is missing and what to ask next.

2. Is the step stable, repeated, and clear?

Use workflow when:

- Remind due follow-ups daily.
- Move fixed-format info into a fixed table.
- Generate weekly basic stats.
- Push status to responsible people.

3. Is this ability used by the team long-term?

Use Codex when:

- Team needs a lead board.
- Customer info search page.
- Multi-role collaboration status.
- Internal generator.
- Early business data tool.

### Tool Choice Flow

```text
找到真实业务动作
→ 写清输入
→ 写清判断标准
→ 写清输出给谁
→ 人工跑三次
→ 再判断 Agent / workflow / Codex
```

Manual first is not backward.

Manual first reveals:

- Which inputs are not unified.
- Which judgments are still feelings.
- Which results are not filled back.
- Which actions are not actually repeated.
- Which parts deserve automation.

### Three Typical Scenarios

Content review:

```text
Comments vary every day. Agent can help classify problems, but operations must confirm and turn high-frequency issues into topics.
```

Lead reminder:

```text
If a table has owner, deadline, current stage, and next action, workflow can remind. If it only says “尽快”, workflow only repeats vagueness.
```

Team dashboard:

```text
If operations, sales, and delivery need the same lead status with filters, permissions, and search, Codex can turn the stable process into a tool.
```

### Two Business Cases

Local store says:

```text
我想自动做复购。
```

Ask first:

- Which product has a repeat cycle?
- When did the customer last consume?
- What signal triggers repeat purchase?
- Who handles it?
- How is the result recorded?

Enterprise service says:

```text
让 AI 自动接待客户。
```

Split it:

- Organize consultation?
- Ask missing information?
- Arrange appointment?
- Generate initial material?
- Judge solution?

The first three may be assisted by AI. The last often needs industry rules and human confirmation.

### Three Checks Before Automation

| Check | Question |
|-|-|
| Input unified? | Are employees using consistent fields and wording? |
| Rules written? | Are stages, A/B/C, and advancement criteria clear? |
| Result filled back? | Do you know whether the reminder or action worked? |

### Human-AI Boundary

Put AI in three positions:

- Organize.
- Suggest.
- Remind.

Put people in three positions:

- Commit.
- Choose.
- Final judgment.

Public line:

```text
人机协作做得好，不是让人越来越闲，而是让人的时间从复制粘贴，转到真正需要经验的地方。
```

## Day 6: 把真实业务拿出来做公开诊断

Core judgment:

```text
没有诊断，工具没有位置。
公开诊断只看业务结构、漏点和第一步动作，不展开客户隐私和完整交付方案。
```

### What This Day Solves

“我想上 AI” is not yet a demand.

It is like saying:

```text
我想装修。
```

You still need to know:

- Is the kitchen leaking?
- Is storage not enough?
- Does the room need replanning?

For AI, ask:

- Where do customers come from?
- Where do leads land?
- How do employees receive?
- How does the result return?
- When does repeat purchase trigger?

### Five Diagnosis Entrances

| Entrance | Question |
|-|-|
| Customer source | Platform content, search, referral, live inquiry, or old customer? |
| Lead location | Comment, DM, employee phone, group, boss memory? |
| Employee reception | What is the first step, what info is missing, who replies, when? |
| Result feedback | Was the result recorded, and why did it advance or not advance? |
| Repeat-purchase trigger | Service cycle, replenishment, renewal, or next demand? |

### Diagnosis Should Be Small

Do not diagnose everything at once.

Public line:

```text
一个小团队第一次只要找到一个最大漏点，并确定一个 7 天能跑的动作。
问题太多，执行就会重新变成等待。
```

### Four Public Diagnosis Questions

1. What is your business loop in one sentence?
2. Which step is most scattered?
3. What must the first table record?
4. What is the first 7-day action?

If these are answered, tool choice becomes natural:

- Comment sorting: Agent.
- Daily reminder: workflow.
- Shared business page: Codex.

### Case 1: Local Store

Boss says:

```text
我们每天也发内容，但是到店的人不稳定。
```

Do not immediately give a content calendar or paid traffic suggestion.

Ask:

- Which content made the customer ask?
- Did they ask location, price, service, or timing?
- Did employees record these questions?
- After the visit, did the team record what the customer cared about?
- Was there a next service or repeat-purchase node?

If the biggest leak is “visited but no service preference or next node,” first action is a light service record:

- Customer source.
- Service item.
- On-site question.
- Next possible need.
- Responsible reminder.

### Case 2: Small Enterprise Service Team

Boss says:

```text
我们有很多咨询，但是销售跟不住。
```

Do not start with CRM price or Agent construction.

Ask where customers scatter:

- Platform DMs.
- Personal phones.
- Group chats.
- Email.
- Meeting notes.

If the leak is handoff, change:

```text
你跟一下。
```

into five facts:

```text
来源、客户原话、当前阶段、主责、下一步和截止时间。
```

“What not to do yet” protects execution.

- Do not build complex system.
